About Repeat Chinese

Repeat Chinese is a free online platform for learning Mandarin Chinese pronunciation. Designed for beginners, it uses the "listen and repeat" method — the most natural way to learn a language.

Our Philosophy

Most Chinese learning websites focus on reading and writing. But language lives in speech. We believe the best way to learn pronunciation is simple: hear a native speaker, then repeat what you heard. Our site automates this process using browser-based text-to-speech (TTS).

Features

  • 📖 18 Lessons — From tones to everyday conversations, 80+ practical sentences
  • 🔊 TTS Pronunciation — No audio downloads needed, works right in your browser
  • 🔄 Auto-repeat Mode — Listen → pause for you to repeat → auto-advance
  • 📝 Mock Exams — Random questions, countdown timer, real-time scoring
  • 🐌 Slow Speed Option — Start slow, speed up as you improve
  • 📊 Progress Tracking — Your progress is saved automatically in your browser
  • 🏆 Score Records — Your best exam score is saved to motivate you

Who Is This For?

  • Absolute beginners starting from zero
  • Self-learners who want structured practice
  • Chinese heritage speakers improving pronunciation
  • HSK test takers needing listening practice

Technical Notes

This site uses the Web Speech API (TTS) for pronunciation. It works best in Chrome or Edge. All data is stored locally in your browser — no account, no sign-up, no data sent to any server.
