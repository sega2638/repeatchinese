Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information — no names, email addresses, or phone numbers. You do not need to sign up or log in to use this website.

2. Local Storage

We use your browser's localStorage to store:

  • Which sentences you have completed (to show progress)
  • Your highest exam score

This data stays only in your browser. It is never sent to any server. You can clear it anytime in your browser settings.

3. Google AdSense

This site uses Google AdSense to show ads. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to collect data for personalized advertising, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages you visit
  • Your IP address (anonymized)
  • Ad interaction data

To learn how Google uses this data, see Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Cookies

This website itself does not set cookies. However, Google AdSense may set cookies for ad delivery. You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect ad personalization but will not affect your use of this site.

5. Third-Party Services

This website uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense — for advertising
  • Web Speech API — browser built-in text-to-speech (no data sent to any server)

6. Children's Privacy

This site is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will remove it immediately.

7. Data Security

Since we do not store any personal data on our servers, there is no risk of data breach from our side. Your learning progress is stored only in your own browser.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Checking back periodically is recommended.
